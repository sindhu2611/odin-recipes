Biryani

Description

Biryani is a spicy and aromatic Indian rice dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and flavorful spices. It is cooked by layering rice and masala together, creating a rich and delicious taste that is popular across India.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Green Chilies
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for some time.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, and spices.
  3. Fry onions until golden brown.
  4. Prepare the masala using onions, tomatoes, chilies, and spices.
  5. Add the marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  6. Boil the rice until it is partially cooked.
  7. Layer the rice over the chicken masala.
  8. Add mint leaves, coriander leaves, and fried onions on top.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and aromatic.
  10. Serve hot with raita or salad.
